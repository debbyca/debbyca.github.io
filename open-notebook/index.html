An open notebook

22 Nov 2015 on notebook

Yay! I've successfully deployed a project on Github.

I'm already well into my graduate school, but it seems that I haven't found my studying pace yet. I need to develop a habit of constantly writing down what I'm learning for better reflection.

Therefore, I think it might be a good idea to start a new blog, which technically puts me in the public eye. The pressure to update this blog might force me to work harder. In short, I voluntarily place myself inside a panopticon under the premise that potential supervision would discipline my behavior.

That was creepy. In a brighter note, this is an open notebook of technology studies. Many scholars have willingly share their research ideas and processes with the public via open notebooks. If anyone happens to stop by, I hope some of the notes would be deemed helpful.

Finally, if not for Mozfest 2015, I wouldn't have a Github account. I love what Mozilla's been doing, and I want to make the web a even better place.

Debby
