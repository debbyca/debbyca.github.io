Notes on the 11/25 Lecture - Fieldnotes

04 December 2015 on Methods

Methods can’t be separated from theories.

Fieldnotes

  • Methodological and Theoretical
  • The “raw material” of the research
  • Where description happens
  • Aide-memoire
  • Archives

  • In the field

    • Disruptive
    • Conspicuous

  • Materiality, it has agency

Types of Data

  • Texts
  • Sounds: songs, music, interviews, ideas, thoughts
  • Images
  • Web… ?

Fieldnotes: an organization

  • Notebooks
    • for interviews and description
    • For technical activities
    • For drawings
    • For genealogies
    • For ideas
  • Those you carry around, those you don’t…
  • Leaflets
    • Proliferate
    • System of “cards” (A5)
    • Maps
    • Local documents
    • “Address book” for index and vocabulary

Fieldnotes and their materiality

  • Paper or Digital?
    • Different types of fragility
    • Different quality of notes
    • Different relations with people
  • Pen? Ink? Others?
  • Instruments: compass, GPS, etc.
  • Transport
  • Supplies (battery?)
  • Back-up
  • Organize for analysis
