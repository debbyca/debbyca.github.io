A Brief Account of the Personal Library

C's living room of the home I visited also served as her personal library. Since she did not acquire additional furniture when she moved in, the tall and short bookshelves must have been used by the previous occupant of the flat as well. There is actually ample historical evidence that the library in the house was used as the living room in the 19th century.
